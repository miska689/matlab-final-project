PWM Generators

What is PWM?

PWM stands for Pulse Width Modulation. It is a technique used to control the amount of power delivered to a load by varying the duty cycle of a digital signal.

Applications

  • Controlling motor speed
  • Dimming LED lights
  • Generating analog signals from digital sources
  • Power regulation in power supplies

Advantages of PWM

  • High efficiency
  • Precise control
  • Compact implementation
